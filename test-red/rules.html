Rules & requirements

Since this is a self-service space, we all rely on each other to keep it running well. A few ground rules keep the rig in good shape for the next driver.

Minimum age

Drivers must be 16 or older. Drivers under 18 need a parent or guardian's consent at time of booking. [Confirm your actual age policy — placeholder text]

Arrive on time

Your session starts and ends at the times you booked. Arriving late doesn't extend your session — the room becomes available to the next booking automatically.

Treat the rig with care

No food or drinks near the equipment. Please don't force the wheel, pedals, or shifter past their natural range of motion.

One driver per session

Sessions are booked for a single driver. Spectators are welcome but should stay clear of the rig while it's in use.

Leave it ready for the next driver

Reset your seat position and turn off any personal profile settings before you leave, so the next booking starts fresh.

Report issues immediately

If something isn't working, use the contact info in your booking confirmation to let us know right away rather than trying to fix it yourself.
